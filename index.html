Procedure

  1. Fill the Enquiry form. Please make sure that the form is filled with all the correct details.
  2. Our team will contact you and take your inputs regarding the design that you require. If you have any special requirements, we can mutually discuss about it's feasibility. After taking your inputs, we will make the specialised design and get it verified from you.
  3. Available payment modes include Bank Transfer, Google Pay (Tez) and Paytm. We will share the details with you through email, sms or a call after the order is finalised. Cheque and Cash Payment is available for only specific locations. Please connect with us on 9304555001 to know more about it.
